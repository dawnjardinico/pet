Meet Weechee!

Weechee is my tyrannical seven-year old shih-tzu! He likes to bark and be mean, but he is cute!



The Weech's Favorite Activities :)

  • Bark at my friends
  • Attempt to bite my friends
  • Give a nasty side-eye
  • Complain
  • Sigh like he works a 9 to 5 job
  • Cuddle
  • Live rent free in our house
  • Play fetch, but instead of returning the toy, he just runs away

If You Want to Take Care of Weechee

    Weechee is a high maintenance dog. He only expects the best treatment (and my family gives it to him because he's cute).

  • Weechee has 3 beds we bought him, however he only sleeps in our bed.
  • He drinks from one of those fancy water fountains that filters the water.
  • He only eats soft treats that are made from meat or jerky, so no biscuit treats or bones.
  • My parents are scared of him getting eaten by a hawk, if he goes outside by himself, so he does his business on his pee pads in the kitchen.
  • His toys are everywhere around the house. He's 7 years old and still doesn't know how to clean up after himself.
  • When it storms or thunders outside, he likes to put on his Batman shirt (previously pictured).
  • Do not leave clothes on the floor, because he will nap or lay down on them, and try to bite you if you try to pick him up.
  • His mortal enemy is a toy called the "Wobble Wag Giggle". Any time he sees it or hears it, he goes into a frenzy and it doesn't stop until my mom hides it from him.
